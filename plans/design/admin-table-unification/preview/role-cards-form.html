PR-1A · Phase 3 wiring · D12 locked layout

Role cards — Contacts multi-type form

D12 locked: every role (Player / Sponsor / Donor / Volunteer / Other) becomes an always-visible card with a toggle. Selected cards expand inline to show role-specific fields. Unselected cards stay collapsed (~55% opacity). No layout shift on toggle. Replaces the 5 invisible checkboxes. Sample contact: Lynne Davenport — Player + Donor selected.

Edit contact — Identity + Roles sections

Identity uses a 1fr/2fr/2fr grid (F12). Roles section renders 5 cards stacked vertically; 2 selected, 3 unselected.
